Application Process

Admission into LYCEE DE NYANZA takes place while students have been approved of having the necessary requirements to become part of our community.

Applications are welcome all through the year. Interviews are scheduled and organized by the administration and communicated to the parents accordingly.

Application is done to our office in working days from Monday to Friday.

You can also apply online via this website by filling the application form. After confirmation of your application, an email will be sent to you.
